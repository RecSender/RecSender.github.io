What is RecSend?

RecSend simplifies the process of managing and sending recommendation letters. Request letters from your recommenders, store them safely, and send them to schools, scholarships, jobs, and internships at your own pace, all from one unified dashboard.

Why RecSend?

  • No more chasing recommenders or managing attachments manually.
  • Secure cloud-based storage of all your recommendation letters.
  • No more waiting, that means submitting anytime, even for last-minute finds
  • Keep track of all your receipts and see all your submissions in one place.
  • One-click sending or uploading via Chrome extension.
