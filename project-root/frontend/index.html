How It Works

1. Submit a Request

Students fill out a simple form with the recommender's info.

2. Recommender Uploads Letter

We send an email to the recommender with a secure upload process.

3. Letter Stored & Forwarded

Letters are encrypted and safely forwarded when needed.

About RecSend

RecSend is an open-source recommendation letter system built for students and faculty. We believe in transparency, privacy, and simplicity. Your documents are encrypted, your email is secure, and your workflow stays distraction-free.

This project was made to eliminate the frustration of chasing emails and lost PDFs. Everything is centralized and efficient—just the way it should be.
